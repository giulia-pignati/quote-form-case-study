04 — Research

What was causing the problem?

Jargon

Confusing terms led to incorrect inputs or missing info.

Length

The form was too long and overwhelming.

Grouping

Questions weren't grouped in an intuitive way.

Responsiveness

Over 60% of users attempted to access a non-responsive form on their phones.

07 — Solution

Mobile-first

→
Designed for the smallest screen first

Over 60% of users accessed the form from their phones. Instead of treating mobile as an afterthought, we designed for it first and scaled up to desktop.

This constraint forced cleaner decisions: one concept per screen, large touch targets, visible progress, and a clear sense of forward motion.
